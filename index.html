Cross Country
Connections

exploring remote interaction

Due to the increased use of remote platforms such as Zoom, our goal is to explore beyond the limitations of current technologies that influence human connections. With two large-scale light installations placed in San Francisco and Pittsburgh, we utilize physical interaction between the users to study how remote collaboration and connection can be more engaging and playful.

This project is funded by a SURG (Small Undergraduate Research Grant) from Carnegie Mellon University for Summer 2021.
